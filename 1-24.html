Math Skills Review: 30 Questions

Practice set covering Reducing, Midpoints, Decimals, Equations, Geometry, and Mixed Numbers.

1. Reduce Before Multiplying

1. \( \frac{5}{9} \times \frac{3}{5} \)
Cancel the 5s. Reduce 3 and 9 (divide by 3).
Answer: \( \frac{1}{3} \)
2. \( \frac{7}{8} \times \frac{4}{7} \)
Cancel the 7s. Reduce 4 and 8 (divide by 4) to 1 and 2.
Answer: \( \frac{1}{2} \)
3. \( \frac{2}{3} \times \frac{9}{10} \)
Reduce 2 and 10 (to 1 and 5). Reduce 9 and 3 (to 3 and 1).
\( \frac{1}{1} \times \frac{3}{5} \)
Answer: \( \frac{3}{5} \)
4. \( \frac{6}{11} \times \frac{11}{12} \)
Cancel the 11s. Reduce 6 and 12 (to 1 and 2).
Answer: \( \frac{1}{2} \)
5. \( \frac{3}{4} \times \frac{8}{15} \)
Reduce 8 and 4 (to 2 and 1). Reduce 3 and 15 (to 1 and 5).
\( \frac{1}{1} \times \frac{2}{5} \)
Answer: \( \frac{2}{5} \)

2. Midpoints & Averages

6. What number is halfway between 10 and 20?
Average: \( \frac{10+20}{2} = \frac{30}{2} \)
Answer: 15
7. Find the midpoint of 0.5 and 1.5.
Add: \( 0.5 + 1.5 = 2.0 \). Divide by 2.
Answer: 1.0
8. What number is halfway between \( \frac{1}{5} \) and \( \frac{3}{5} \)?
Add numerators: \( 1+3=4 \). Sum is \( \frac{4}{5} \).
Divide by 2: \( \frac{4}{5} \div 2 = \frac{2}{5} \).
Answer: \( \frac{2}{5} \)
9. Find the number halfway between \( \frac{1}{2} \) and \( \frac{1}{4} \).
Common denominator 8: \( \frac{4}{8} \) and \( \frac{2}{8} \).
Add: \( \frac{6}{8} \). Divide by 2: \( \frac{3}{8} \).
Answer: \( \frac{3}{8} \)
10. What is the average of 1.2, 1.4, and 1.6?
Sum: \( 1.2 + 1.4 + 1.6 = 4.2 \).
Divide by 3: \( 4.2 \div 3 = 1.4 \).
Answer: 1.4

3. Dividing by Decimals

11. \( 4 \div 0.2 \)
Move decimal right 1 spot: \( 40 \div 2 \).
Answer: 20
12. \( 1.5 \div 0.5 \)
Move decimal: \( 15 \div 5 \).
Answer: 3
13. \( 0.24 \div 0.04 \)
Move decimal right 2 spots: \( 24 \div 4 \).
Answer: 6
14. \( 36 \div 0.6 \)
Move decimal: \( 360 \div 6 \).
Answer: 60
15. \( 0.9 \div 0.03 \)
Move decimal 2 spots: \( 90 \div 3 \).
Answer: 30

4. Solving Fraction Equations

16. Solve for \( x \): \( x - \frac{2}{7} = \frac{3}{7} \)
Add \( \frac{2}{7} \) to both sides.
\( x = \frac{3}{7} + \frac{2}{7} \)
Answer: \( \frac{5}{7} \)
17. Solve for \( y \): \( \frac{1}{2} + y = \frac{5}{6} \)
Subtract \( \frac{1}{2} \) (which is \( \frac{3}{6} \)) from \( \frac{5}{6} \).
\( \frac{5}{6} - \frac{3}{6} = \frac{2}{6} \)
Answer: \( \frac{1}{3} \)
18. Solve for \( n \): \( n - \frac{3}{8} = \frac{1}{2} \)
Convert \( \frac{1}{2} \) to \( \frac{4}{8} \). Add \( \frac{3}{8} \).
Answer: \( \frac{7}{8} \)
19. Solve for \( w \): \( 1 - w = \frac{1}{3} \)
\( w = 1 - \frac{1}{3} \).
\( \frac{3}{3} - \frac{1}{3} \)
Answer: \( \frac{2}{3} \)
20. Solve for \( k \): \( k + \frac{1}{5} = \frac{4}{5} \)
Subtract \( \frac{1}{5} \) from both sides.
Answer: \( \frac{3}{5} \)

5. Geometry: Solids

21. How many vertices does a cube have?
A cube has 4 corners on top and 4 on bottom.
Answer: 8 vertices
22. How many faces does a rectangular prism have?
Top, bottom, front, back, left, right.
Answer: 6 faces
23. How many edges does a triangular pyramid have?
3 edges on the base, 3 edges meeting at the top point.
Answer: 6 edges
24. What shape is the base of a "square pyramid"?
As the name suggests...
Answer: A square
25. A triangular prism has how many faces?
2 triangular bases + 3 rectangular sides.
Answer: 5 faces

6. Multiplying Mixed Numbers

26. \( 1\frac{1}{2} \times 4 \)
Convert to improper: \( \frac{3}{2} \times \frac{4}{1} \).
Cancel 2 and 4. \( 3 \times 2 = 6 \).
Answer: 6
27. \( 2\frac{1}{3} \times 1\frac{1}{2} \)
Convert: \( \frac{7}{3} \times \frac{3}{2} \).
Cancel the 3s. Result is \( \frac{7}{2} \).
Answer: \( 3\frac{1}{2} \)
28. \( 3\frac{1}{3} \times \frac{3}{5} \)
Convert: \( \frac{10}{3} \times \frac{3}{5} \).
Cancel 3s. Reduce 10 and 5 (result 2).
Answer: 2
29. \( 1\frac{1}{4} \times 1\frac{3}{5} \)
Convert: \( \frac{5}{4} \times \frac{8}{5} \).
Cancel 5s. Reduce 8 and 4 (result 2).
Answer: 2
30. \( 5\frac{1}{2} \times 2 \)
Convert: \( \frac{11}{2} \times \frac{2}{1} \).
Cancel 2s.
Answer: 11
