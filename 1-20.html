j. Sarah has \( 3 \frac{3}{4} \) hours to finish 5 tasks. If she divides her time equally, what fraction of an hour is spent on each?
Answer: \( \frac{3}{4} \) hour
\( 3 \frac{3}{4} = \frac{15}{4} \)
\( \frac{15}{4} \div 5 = \frac{15}{4} \times \frac{1}{5} = \frac{3}{4} \)

Written Practice (Similar Problems)

1. Find the difference between the sum of \( \frac{1}{3} \) and \( \frac{1}{6} \) and the product of \( \frac{1}{3} \) and \( \frac{1}{6} \).
Answer: \( \frac{4}{9} \)
Sum: \( \frac{2}{6} + \frac{1}{6} = \frac{3}{6} = \frac{1}{2} \)
Product: \( \frac{1}{18} \)
Diff: \( \frac{9}{18} - \frac{1}{18} = \frac{8}{18} = \frac{4}{9} \)
2. Tom ran a mile in 5 minutes 20 seconds. How many total seconds is that?
Answer: 320 seconds
\( 5 \times 60 = 300 \)
\( 300 + 20 = 320 \)
3. A table is 5 feet \( 2\frac{1}{2} \) inches long. How many inches is that?
Answer: \( 62\frac{1}{2} \) inches
\( 5 \text{ ft} \times 12 = 60 \text{ in} \)
\( 60 + 2\frac{1}{2} = 62\frac{1}{2} \)
6. In 5 games, the team scored 85 points. What was the average score?
Answer: 17 points
\( 85 \div 5 = 17 \)
7. Write the prime factorizations of 18 and 60. Then reduce \( \frac{18}{60} \).
Answer: \( \frac{3}{10} \)
18: \( 2 \cdot 3 \cdot 3 \)
60: \( 2 \cdot 2 \cdot 3 \cdot 5 \)
Reduce: Divide both by 6.
8. Find \( m \): \( m - 4\frac{2}{5} = 2\frac{3}{10} \)
Answer: \( 6\frac{7}{10} \)
\( m = 2\frac{3}{10} + 4\frac{4}{10} = 6\frac{7}{10} \)
10. Solve for \( d \): \( 50d = 0.25 \)
Answer: \( 0.005 \)
\( d = \frac{0.25}{50} = \frac{25}{5000} = \frac{1}{200} = 0.005 \)
15. What is the area of a rectangle 6 inches long and \( 2\frac{1}{2} \) inches wide?

Answer: 15 sq inches
\( 6 \times 2.5 = 15 \)
18. ANALYZE: The product \( 4.5 \times 100 \) is equal to:
A) \( 4500 \div 10 \) B) \( 45 \div 10 \)
Answer: A) \( 4500 \div 10 \)
\( 4.5 \times 100 = 450 \)
Option A is \( 450 \). Option B is \( 4.5 \).
19. Find the sum of 1245, 2810, and 5950 to the nearest thousand.
Answer: 10,000
Exact sum: 10,005. Rounds to 10,000.
21. The perimeter of a square is 3.2 meters. Find the side length and area.

Side: 0.8 m | Area: 0.64 sq m
\( 3.2 \div 4 = 0.8 \)
\( 0.8 \times 0.8 = 0.64 \)
22. What is the tax on a $25,000 car if the rate is 6%?
Answer: $1,500
\( 25,000 \times 0.06 = 1,500 \)
23. If the probability of rain is 20%, what is the probability it will NOT rain?
Answer: 80% (or \( \frac{4}{5} \))
\( 100\% - 20\% = 80\% \)
27. An angle is 110 degrees. Is it acute, obtuse, or right?

Answer: Obtuse
Angles greater than 90° and less than 180° are obtuse.
30. Sunrise is 6:10 AM and Sunset is 8:25 PM. How long is the day?
Answer: 14 hours 15 minutes
6:10 AM to 6:10 PM = 12 hours.
6:10 PM to 8:10 PM = 2 hours.
8:10 PM to 8:25 PM = 15 mins.
Total: 14 hrs 15 mins.
