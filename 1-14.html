Lesson Practice

Write the prime factorization of both the numerator and the denominator of each fraction. Then reduce each fraction.

a. \(\frac{875}{1000}\)
Prime Factorization:
Numerator (875): \(875 = 5 \times 175 = 5 \times 5 \times 35 = 5 \times 5 \times 5 \times 7 = 5^3 \times 7\)
Denominator (1000): \(1000 = 10^3 = (2 \times 5)^3 = 2^3 \times 5^3\)
Reduction:
$$ \frac{5^3 \times 7}{2^3 \times 5^3} $$ The \(5^3\) terms cancel out. $$ \frac{7}{2^3} = \frac{7}{8} $$
Answer: \(\frac{7}{8}\)
b. \(\frac{48}{400}\)
Prime Factorization:
Numerator (48): \(16 \times 3 = 2^4 \times 3\)
Denominator (400): \(4 \times 100 = 2^2 \times 10^2 = 2^2 \times (2 \times 5)^2 = 2^2 \times 2^2 \times 5^2 = 2^4 \times 5^2\)
Reduction:
$$ \frac{2^4 \times 3}{2^4 \times 5^2} $$ The \(2^4\) terms cancel out. $$ \frac{3}{5^2} = \frac{3}{25} $$
Answer: \(\frac{3}{25}\)
c. \(\frac{125}{500}\)
Prime Factorization:
Numerator (125): \(5^3\)
Denominator (500): \(5 \times 100 = 5 \times 10^2 = 5 \times (2^2 \times 5^2) = 2^2 \times 5^3\)
Reduction:
$$ \frac{5^3}{2^2 \times 5^3} $$ The \(5^3\) terms cancel out, leaving 1 in the numerator. $$ \frac{1}{2^2} = \frac{1}{4} $$
Answer: \(\frac{1}{4}\)
d. \(\frac{36}{81}\)
Prime Factorization:
Numerator (36): \(4 \times 9 = 2^2 \times 3^2\)
Denominator (81): \(9 \times 9 = 3^2 \times 3^2 = 3^4\)
Reduction:
$$ \frac{2^2 \times 3^2}{3^4} $$ Subtract exponents for base 3: \(4 - 2 = 2\) in the denominator. $$ \frac{2^2}{3^2} = \frac{4}{9} $$
Answer: \(\frac{4}{9}\)

Written Practice

1. Ribbon Collage Problem

Allison needs 2 yards blue, \(\frac{1}{2}\) yard yellow, and \(\frac{3}{4}\) yard green. Ribbon costs $2 a yard.

Total Length:
$$ 2 + \frac{1}{2} + \frac{3}{4} = 2 + 0.5 + 0.75 = 3.25 \text{ yards} $$ (Alternatively: \(2 + \frac{2}{4} + \frac{3}{4} = 2 + \frac{5}{4} = 3 \frac{1}{4}\) yards)

Total Cost:
$$ 3.25 \times \$2.00 = \$6.50 $$
Answer: $6.50
2. Estimate: Nautical vs. Standard Mile

Standard mile = 5280 ft. Nautical mile ≈ 6080 ft. About how much longer is a nautical mile?

Subtract the standard mile from the nautical mile: $$ 6080 - 5280 = 800 \text{ feet} $$
Answer: About 800 feet
3. Verify Division

Original: $1.50 ÷ $0.05. Marcus multiplied both by 100.

Equivalent problem: $$ \frac{1.50 \times 100}{0.05 \times 100} = \frac{150}{5} $$ $$ 150 \div 5 = 30 $$
Equivalent problem: 150 ÷ 5; Quotient: 30
4. Find Unknown: \(6\text{ cm} + k = 11\text{ cm}\)
Subtract 6 cm from both sides: $$ k = 11 - 6 $$ $$ k = 5 $$
Answer: k = 5 cm
5. Find Unknown: \(8g = 9.6\)
Divide both sides by 8: $$ g = \frac{9.6}{8} $$ $$ g = 1.2 $$
Answer: g = 1.2
6. Find Unknown: \(\frac{7}{10} - w = \frac{1}{2}\)
Convert \(\frac{1}{2}\) to \(\frac{5}{10}\). $$ \frac{7}{10} - w = \frac{5}{10} $$ $$ w = \frac{7}{10} - \frac{5}{10} = \frac{2}{10} $$ Reduce: $$ w = \frac{1}{5} $$
Answer: w = \(\frac{1}{5}\)
7. Find Unknown: \(\frac{3}{5} = \frac{n}{100}\)
To get from 5 to 100, multiply by 20. Do the same to the numerator. $$ 3 \times 20 = 60 $$ So, \(n = 60\).
Answer: n = 60
8. Quadrilateral Perimeter

Perimeter = 172 inches. What is the average length? Can we know the type?

Average Length: Divide perimeter by 4 sides. $$ 172 \div 4 = 43 \text{ inches} $$ Type of Quadrilateral: Knowing only the perimeter (sum of sides) does not define the shape. It could be a square, a rectangle, a trapezoid, etc.
Answer: 43 inches; No, perimeter alone does not determine shape.
9. Simplify: \(\$100.00 - (\$46.75 + \$9.68)\)
First, add the values in the parentheses: $$ 46.75 + 9.68 = 56.43 $$ Next, subtract from 100: $$ 100.00 - 56.43 = 43.57 $$
Answer: $43.57
10. Simplify: \((2 \times 0.3) - (0.2 \times 0.3)\)
$$ (0.6) - (0.06) $$ $$ 0.60 - 0.06 = 0.54 $$
Answer: 0.54
11. Analyze: \(4\frac{1}{4} - 2\frac{7}{8}\)
Find common denominator (8): $$ 4\frac{2}{8} - 2\frac{7}{8} $$ Borrow 1 from the 4 (making it \(3 + \frac{8}{8}\)): $$ 3\frac{10}{8} - 2\frac{7}{8} $$ Subtract integers and fractions: $$ (3-2) = 1, \quad (\frac{10}{8} - \frac{7}{8}) = \frac{3}{8} $$
Answer: \(1\frac{3}{8}\)
12. Analyze: \(2\frac{2}{3} \times \sqrt{9}\)
Convert mixed number to improper fraction: \(2\frac{2}{3} = \frac{8}{3}\)
Calculate root: \(\sqrt{9} = 3\) $$ \frac{8}{3} \times 3 = \frac{24}{3} = 8 $$
Answer: 8
13. Add: \(3\frac{1}{3} + 2\frac{3}{4}\)
Common denominator is 12. $$ 3\frac{4}{12} + 2\frac{9}{12} = 5\frac{13}{12} $$ Convert improper fraction part: \(\frac{13}{12} = 1\frac{1}{12}\) $$ 5 + 1\frac{1}{12} = 6\frac{1}{12} $$
Answer: \(6\frac{1}{12}\)
14. Multiply: \(1\frac{1}{3} \times 2\frac{1}{4}\)
Convert to improper fractions: $$ \frac{4}{3} \times \frac{9}{4} $$ Cancel the 4s: $$ \frac{1}{3} \times \frac{9}{1} = \frac{9}{3} = 3 $$
Answer: 3
15. Divide: \(1.44 \div 60\)
$$ \frac{1.44}{60} = \frac{1.44}{6 \times 10} $$ $$ 1.44 \div 6 = 0.24 $$ $$ 0.24 \div 10 = 0.024 $$
Answer: 0.024
16. Divide: \(\$6.00 \div \$0.15\)
Move decimals 2 places right for both: $$ 600 \div 15 $$ $$ 15 \times 4 = 60 \rightarrow 15 \times 40 = 600 $$
Answer: 40
17. Division Word Problem

Five dollars divided evenly among 4 people.

$$ 5.00 \div 4 = 1.25 $$
Answer: $1.25 each
18. Regular Quadrilateral

Area is 100 sq inches. What is its perimeter? Name of the quadrilateral?

A "regular quadrilateral" has equal sides and angles. This is a Square.
Area = \(s^2 = 100\). So side \(s = 10\).
Perimeter = \(4 \times s = 4 \times 10 = 40\).
Name: Square; Perimeter: 40 inches
19. Prime Factorization and Reduction

Reduce \(\frac{625}{1000}\) using prime factorization.

\(625 = 25 \times 25 = 5^2 \times 5^2 = 5^4\)
\(1000 = 10 \times 100 = 10^3 = (2 \times 5)^3 = 2^3 \times 5^3\)
Reduction: $$ \frac{5^4}{2^3 \times 5^3} = \frac{5}{2^3} = \frac{5}{8} $$
Answer: \(\frac{5}{8}\)
20. Area of Rectangle

Dimensions: \(1\frac{1}{2}\) in by \(\frac{3}{4}\) in.

Convert to improper: \(1\frac{1}{2} = \frac{3}{2}\). $$ \text{Area} = \frac{3}{2} \times \frac{3}{4} = \frac{9}{8} $$ $$ \frac{9}{8} = 1\frac{1}{8} \text{ sq in} $$
Answer: \(1\frac{1}{8}\) sq in.
21. Piano Keys

36 of 88 keys are black. What fraction are black?

Fraction: \(\frac{36}{88}\)
Divide numerator and denominator by 4: $$ \frac{36 \div 4}{88 \div 4} = \frac{9}{22} $$
Answer: \(\frac{9}{22}\)

26. Estimate: Clock Hand Travel

Minute hand tip is 6 inches from center. How far does it travel in one hour?

Radius \(r = 6\) inches.
One hour = One full circumference.
$$ C = 2 \pi r \approx 2 \times 3.14 \times 6 $$ $$ 12 \times 3.14 = 37.68 $$ Round to nearest inch.
Answer: 38 inches

29. Probability

Probability of rolling a prime number with one number cube (die).

Possible outcomes: {1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6}

Probability = \(\frac{\text{favorable}}{\text{total}}\) $$ \frac{3}{6} = \frac{1}{2} $$
Answer: \(\frac{1}{2}\)
