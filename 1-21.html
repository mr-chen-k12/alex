Math Practice Set 2

Problem 1
What is the difference between the sum of \(\frac{2}{3}\) and \(\frac{1}{6}\) and the product of \(\frac{2}{3}\) and \(\frac{1}{6}\)?
Answer: \(\frac{13}{18}\)

Sum: \(\frac{4}{6} + \frac{1}{6} = \frac{5}{6} = \frac{15}{18}\)
Product: \(\frac{2}{18} = \frac{1}{9}\)
Difference: \(\frac{15}{18} - \frac{2}{18} = \frac{13}{18}\)
Problem 2
A song lasts 4 minutes and 12 seconds. How many total seconds is that?
Answer: 252 seconds

\(4 \times 60 = 240\)
\(240 + 12 = 252\)
Problem 3
A rope is 6 feet \(3\frac{1}{2}\) inches long. Convert this length entirely to inches.
Answer: \(75\frac{1}{2}\) inches

\(6 \text{ ft} \times 12 = 72 \text{ in}\)
\(72 + 3\frac{1}{2} = 75\frac{1}{2}\)
Problem 4
Calculate: \(2\frac{2}{5} \div 1\frac{1}{5}\)
Answer: \(2\)

\(\frac{12}{5} \div \frac{6}{5} = \frac{12}{5} \times \frac{5}{6} = \frac{12}{6} = 2\)
Problem 5
Calculate: \(8 \div 2\frac{2}{3}\)
Answer: \(3\)

\(8 \div \frac{8}{3} = \frac{8}{1} \times \frac{3}{8} = 3\)
Problem 6
Over 5 days, a shop sold a total of 150 items. What was the average number of items sold per day?
Answer: 30 items

\(150 \div 5 = 30\)
Problem 7
Write the prime factorization of 45 and 75. Then reduce \(\frac{45}{75}\).
Answer: \(\frac{3}{5}\)

45: \(3 \times 3 \times 5\)
75: \(3 \times 5 \times 5\)
Reduce: Divide both by 15.
Problem 8
Find \(m\): \(m - 3\frac{1}{2} = 4\frac{3}{4}\)
Answer: \(8\frac{1}{4}\)

\(m = 4\frac{3}{4} + 3\frac{2}{4} = 7\frac{5}{4} = 8\frac{1}{4}\)
Problem 9
Find \(n\): \(n + 1\frac{1}{3} = 3\frac{5}{6}\)
Answer: \(2\frac{1}{2}\)

\(n = 3\frac{5}{6} - 1\frac{2}{6} = 2\frac{3}{6} = 2\frac{1}{2}\)
Problem 10
Solve for \(k\): \(40k = 1.2\)
Answer: 0.03

\(k = \frac{1.2}{40} = \frac{12}{400} = \frac{3}{100} = 0.03\)
Problem 11
Solve for \(x\): \(\frac{7}{20} = \frac{x}{100}\)
Answer: 35

\(20 \times 5 = 100\), so \(7 \times 5 = 35\).
Problem 12
Calculate: \(7\frac{1}{4} - 2\frac{3}{4}\)
Answer: \(4\frac{1}{2}\)

Borrow from 7: \(6\frac{5}{4} - 2\frac{3}{4} = 4\frac{2}{4} = 4\frac{1}{2}\)
Problem 13
Calculate: \(2\frac{1}{2} \times 3\frac{1}{3}\)
Answer: \(8\frac{1}{3}\)

\(\frac{5}{2} \times \frac{10}{3} = \frac{50}{6} = \frac{25}{3} = 8\frac{1}{3}\)
Problem 14
Calculate: \(5\frac{1}{3} \div 2\)
Answer: \(2\frac{2}{3}\)

\(\frac{16}{3} \times \frac{1}{2} = \frac{8}{3} = 2\frac{2}{3}\)
Problem 15
What is the area of a rectangle 5 inches long and \(2\frac{1}{4}\) inches wide?
Answer: \(11\frac{1}{4}\) sq in

\(5 \times \frac{9}{4} = \frac{45}{4} = 11\frac{1}{4}\)
Problem 16

Answer: 3

\(7.0 - 4.0 = 3\)
Problem 17
Calculate: \(14.4 \div 0.6\)
Answer: 24

\(144 \div 6 = 24\)
Problem 18
The product \(2.8 \times 10\) equals which of the following?
A) \(280 \div 10\)    B) \(2800 \div 10\)
Answer: A) \(280 \div 10\)

\(2.8 \times 10 = 28\).
Option A is \(28\). Option B is \(280\).
Problem 19
Find the sum of 3120, 4850, and 1990 to the nearest thousand.
Answer: 10,000

Sum is 9,960. Rounded to nearest thousand is 10,000.
Problem 20

Problem 21
The perimeter of a square is 6 meters. Find the side length and area.
Side: 1.5 m
Area: 2.25 sq m

\(6 \div 4 = 1.5\)
\(1.5 \times 1.5 = 2.25\)
Problem 22
What is the tax on a $12,000 car if the tax rate is 7%?
Answer: $840

\(12,000 \times 0.07 = 840\)
Problem 23
If the probability of winning is \( \frac{1}{4} \), what is the probability of NOT winning?
Answer: \(\frac{3}{4}\)

\(1 - \frac{1}{4} = \frac{3}{4}\)
Problem 24
Why is a semicircle not considered a polygon?
Answer: Because it has a curved edge. Polygons must have only straight sides.
Problem 25

Answer: Equal (=)

Both equal 2.

Problem 30
A worker started at 8:30 a.m. and finished at 3:15 p.m. How long did they work?
Answer: 6 hours 45 minutes

8:30 to 2:30 is 6 hours.
2:30 to 3:15 is 45 minutes.
