Math Practice Set 3

Question 1
Find the difference between the sum of \(\frac{3}{5}\) and \(\frac{1}{10}\) and their product.
Answer: \(\frac{16}{25}\) (or \(\frac{32}{50}\))
Sum: \(\frac{6}{10} + \frac{1}{10} = \frac{7}{10}\)
Product: \(\frac{3}{50}\)
Diff: \(\frac{35}{50} - \frac{3}{50} = \frac{32}{50} = \frac{16}{25}\)
Question 2
A sprinter ran a race in 1 minute 15 seconds. How many seconds is that?
Answer: 75 seconds
\(60 + 15 = 75\)
Question 3
A board is 3 feet and \(4\frac{1}{2}\) inches long. How many total inches is it?
Answer: \(40\frac{1}{2}\) inches
\(3 \times 12 = 36\).
\(36 + 4.5 = 40.5\)
Question 4
Calculate: \(4\frac{1}{5} \div 2\frac{1}{10}\)
Answer: 2
\(\frac{21}{5} \div \frac{21}{10} = \frac{21}{5} \times \frac{10}{21} = 2\)
Question 5
Calculate: \(10 \div 3\frac{1}{3}\)
Answer: 3
\(10 \div \frac{10}{3} = 10 \times \frac{3}{10} = 3\)
Question 6
In 4 basketball games, the team scored 204 points. What was the average score?
Answer: 51 points
\(204 \div 4 = 51\)
Question 7
Prime factorize 30 and 42, then reduce \(\frac{30}{42}\).
Answer: \(\frac{5}{7}\)
30: \(2 \cdot 3 \cdot 5\)
42: \(2 \cdot 3 \cdot 7\)
Cancel \(2 \cdot 3\) (which is 6).
Question 8

Answer: \(8\frac{5}{8}\)
\(y = 2\frac{3}{8} + 6\frac{2}{8} = 8\frac{5}{8}\)
Question 9

Question 10
