Topic: Percent of a Number & Reduced Fractions (Like #27)

Task: 1. Convert the percent to a reduced fraction. 2. Use the fraction (or decimal) to calculate the amount.

1. Sarah made 20% of the 50 shots she took in basketball.
a. Write 20% as a reduced fraction.
b. How many shots did she make?
Show Answer
a. \( \frac{20}{100} = \frac{1}{5} \) b. \( \frac{1}{5} \times 50 = 10 \) Answer: 10 shots
2. The weather forecast was correct 40% of the last 20 days.
a. Write 40% as a reduced fraction.
b. How many days was it correct?
Show Answer
a. \( \frac{40}{100} = \frac{2}{5} \) b. \( \frac{2}{5} \times 20 = \frac{40}{5} = 8 \) Answer: 8 days
3. Mark saved 25% of his $80 allowance.
a. Write 25% as a reduced fraction.
b. How much money did he save?
Show Answer
a. \( \frac{25}{100} = \frac{1}{4} \) b. \( \frac{1}{4} \times 80 = 20 \) Answer: $20
4. A tank is 50% full. The tank holds 120 gallons.
a. Write 50% as a reduced fraction.
b. How many gallons are in the tank?
Show Answer
a. \( \frac{50}{100} = \frac{1}{2} \) b. \( \frac{1}{2} \times 120 = 60 \) Answer: 60 gallons
5. 75% of the 16 students in the club are girls.
a. Write 75% as a reduced fraction.
b. How many students are girls?
Show Answer
a. \( \frac{75}{100} = \frac{3}{4} \) b. \( \frac{3}{4} \times 16 = 3 \times 4 = 12 \) Answer: 12 students
6. John finished 10% of a 60-mile race.
a. Write 10% as a reduced fraction.
b. How many miles did he run?
Show Answer
a. \( \frac{10}{100} = \frac{1}{10} \) b. \( \frac{1}{10} \times 60 = 6 \) Answer: 6 miles
7. 60% of the 30 animals at the shelter are dogs.
a. Write 60% as a reduced fraction.
b. How many dogs are there?
Show Answer
a. \( \frac{60}{100} = \frac{3}{5} \) b. \( \frac{3}{5} \times 30 = 3 \times 6 = 18 \) Answer: 18 dogs
8. 5% of the 200 tickets were sold.
a. Write 5% as a reduced fraction.
b. How many tickets were sold?
Show Answer
a. \( \frac{5}{100} = \frac{1}{20} \) b. \( \frac{1}{20} \times 200 = 10 \) Answer: 10 tickets
9. 15% of the 40 items were returned.
a. Write 15% as a reduced fraction.
b. How many items were returned?
Show Answer
a. \( \frac{15}{100} = \frac{3}{20} \) b. \( \frac{3}{20} \times 40 = 3 \times 2 = 6 \) Answer: 6 items
10. 80% of the 50 questions were easy.
a. Write 80% as a reduced fraction.
b. How many questions were easy?
Show Answer
a. \( \frac{80}{100} = \frac{4}{5} \) b. \( \frac{4}{5} \times 50 = 40 \) Answer: 40 questions
