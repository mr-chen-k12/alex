Targeted Math Practice

Practice specific skills based on problems 1, 4, 8, 10, 21, and 30.

Type #1: Operations with Fractions (Sum, Product, Difference)

Focus: Finding the difference between the sum and product of two fractions.

1.1) What is the difference between the sum of \(\frac{1}{3}\) and \(\frac{1}{6}\) and the product of \(\frac{1}{3}\) and \(\frac{1}{6}\)?
Answer: \(\frac{4}{9}\)
Sum: \(\frac{2}{6} + \frac{1}{6} = \frac{3}{6} = \frac{1}{2}\)
Product: \(\frac{1}{18}\)
Diff: \(\frac{9}{18} - \frac{1}{18} = \frac{8}{18} = \frac{4}{9}\)
1.2) Find the difference between the sum involving \(\frac{3}{4}\) and \(\frac{1}{2}\) and their product.
Answer: \(\frac{7}{8}\)
Sum: \(\frac{3}{4} + \frac{2}{4} = \frac{5}{4}\)
Product: \(\frac{3}{8}\)
Diff: \(\frac{10}{8} - \frac{3}{8} = \frac{7}{8}\)
1.3) Calculate: \((\frac{2}{5} + \frac{1}{2}) - (\frac{2}{5} \times \frac{1}{2})\)
Answer: \(\frac{7}{10}\)
Sum: \(\frac{9}{10}\)
Product: \(\frac{2}{10}\)
Diff: \(\frac{7}{10}\)
1.4) What is the difference between the sum of \(\frac{1}{4}\) and \(\frac{1}{5}\) and the product of \(\frac{1}{4}\) and \(\frac{1}{5}\)?
Answer: \(\frac{2}{5}\)
Sum: \(\frac{9}{20}\)
Product: \(\frac{1}{20}\)
Diff: \(\frac{8}{20} = \frac{2}{5}\)
1.5) Find the difference between the sum and product of \(\frac{2}{3}\) and \(\frac{3}{4}\).
Answer: \(\frac{11}{12}\)
Sum: \(\frac{17}{12}\)
Product: \(\frac{6}{12} = \frac{1}{2}\)
Diff: \(\frac{17}{12} - \frac{6}{12} = \frac{11}{12}\)

Type #4: Division of Mixed Numbers

Focus: Converting mixed numbers to improper fractions and multiplying by the reciprocal.

4.1) Calculate: \(1\frac{1}{4} \div 2\frac{1}{2}\)
Answer: \(\frac{1}{2}\)
\(\frac{5}{4} \div \frac{5}{2} = \frac{5}{4} \times \frac{2}{5} = \frac{10}{20} = \frac{1}{2}\)
4.2) Calculate: \(3\frac{1}{3} \div 1\frac{2}{3}\)
Answer: \(2\)
\(\frac{10}{3} \div \frac{5}{3} = \frac{10}{3} \times \frac{3}{5} = 2\)
4.3) Calculate: \(2\frac{1}{5} \div 1\frac{1}{10}\)
Answer: \(2\)
\(\frac{11}{5} \div \frac{11}{10} = \frac{11}{5} \times \frac{10}{11} = 2\)
4.4) Calculate: \(5\frac{1}{2} \div 1\frac{3}{8}\)
Answer: \(4\)
\(\frac{11}{2} \div \frac{11}{8} = \frac{11}{2} \times \frac{8}{11} = \frac{8}{2} = 4\)
4.5) Calculate: \(1\frac{7}{8} \div 1\frac{1}{4}\)
Answer: \(1\frac{1}{2}\)
\(\frac{15}{8} \div \frac{5}{4} = \frac{15}{8} \times \frac{4}{5} = \frac{3}{2} = 1\frac{1}{2}\)

Type #8: Solving Equations with Mixed Numbers

Focus: Solving for a variable involving subtraction of mixed numbers.

8.1) Find \(m\): \(m - 2\frac{1}{2} = 3\frac{3}{4}\)
Answer: \(m = 6\frac{1}{4}\)
\(m = 3\frac{3}{4} + 2\frac{2}{4} = 5\frac{5}{4} = 6\frac{1}{4}\)
8.2) Find \(x\): \(x - 4\frac{1}{3} = 1\frac{5}{6}\)
Answer: \(x = 6\frac{1}{6}\)
\(x = 1\frac{5}{6} + 4\frac{2}{6} = 5\frac{7}{6} = 6\frac{1}{6}\)
8.3) Find \(y\): \(y - 1\frac{3}{8} = 2\frac{1}{2}\)
Answer: \(y = 3\frac{7}{8}\)
\(y = 2\frac{4}{8} + 1\frac{3}{8} = 3\frac{7}{8}\)
8.4) Find \(a\): \(a - 5\frac{2}{5} = 3\frac{7}{10}\)
Answer: \(a = 9\frac{1}{10}\)
\(a = 3\frac{7}{10} + 5\frac{4}{10} = 8\frac{11}{10} = 9\frac{1}{10}\)
8.5) Find \(b\): \(b - 3\frac{1}{4} = 1\frac{2}{3}\)
Answer: \(b = 4\frac{11}{12}\)
\(b = 1\frac{8}{12} + 3\frac{3}{12} = 4\frac{11}{12}\)

Type #10: Solving Decimal Equations

Focus: Isolating variables by dividing decimals or integers.

10.1) Solve for \(d\): \(20d = 0.5\)
Answer: \(d = 0.025\)
\(d = \frac{0.5}{20} = \frac{5}{200} = \frac{1}{40} = 0.025\)
10.2) Solve for \(x\): \(0.4x = 1.2\)
Answer: \(x = 3\)
\(x = 1.2 \div 0.4 = 3\)
10.3) Solve for \(y\): \(15y = 4.5\)
Answer: \(y = 0.3\)
\(y = 4.5 \div 15 = 0.3\)
10.4) Solve for \(z\): \(0.05z = 10\)
Answer: \(z = 200\)
\(z = 10 \div 0.05 = 200\)
10.5) Solve for \(m\): \(50m = 0.1\)
Answer: \(m = 0.002\)
\(m = \frac{0.1}{50} = \frac{1}{500} = 0.002\)

Type #21: Square Perimeter and Area

Focus: Calculating side length from perimeter, then calculating area.

21.1) The perimeter of a square is 3.6 meters. a) How long is each side? b) What is the area?
Side: 0.9 m
Area: 0.81 sq m
(3.6 / 4 = 0.9; 0.9 * 0.9 = 0.81)
21.2) The perimeter of a square is 20 inches. a) How long is each side? b) What is the area?
Side: 5 in
Area: 25 sq in
21.3) The perimeter of a square is 4.8 cm. a) How long is each side? b) What is the area?
Side: 1.2 cm
Area: 1.44 sq cm
(4.8 / 4 = 1.2; 1.2 * 1.2 = 1.44)
21.4) The perimeter of a square is 1 meter. a) How long is each side (in decimals)? b) What is the area?
Side: 0.25 m
Area: 0.0625 sq m
21.5) The perimeter of a square is 12 feet. a) How long is each side? b) What is the area?
Side: 3 ft
Area: 9 sq ft

Type #30: Elapsed Time

Focus: Calculating hours and minutes between two times.

30.1) Sunrise was at 6:15 a.m. and sunset was at 7:30 p.m. How long was the day?
Answer: 13 hours, 15 minutes
6:15am to 6:15pm = 12 hours.
6:15pm to 7:30pm = 1 hour 15 minutes.
Total = 13 hrs 15 mins.
30.2) A movie started at 1:45 p.m. and ended at 4:10 p.m. How long was the movie?
Answer: 2 hours, 25 minutes
1:45 to 3:45 = 2 hours.
3:45 to 4:10 = 25 minutes.
30.3) School starts at 8:10 a.m. and ends at 2:50 p.m. How long is the school day?
Answer: 6 hours, 40 minutes
8:10 to 2:10 = 6 hours.
2:10 to 2:50 = 40 minutes.
30.4) A train departed at 10:30 a.m. and arrived at 1:15 p.m. How long was the trip?
Answer: 2 hours, 45 minutes
10:30 to 12:30 = 2 hours.
12:30 to 1:15 = 45 minutes.
30.5) A worker clocked in at 7:55 a.m. and clocked out at 4:25 p.m. How long was their shift?
Answer: 8 hours, 30 minutes
7:55 to 3:55 = 8 hours.
3:55 to 4:25 = 30 minutes.
