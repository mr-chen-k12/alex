Topics #4 & #6: Reducing Before Multiplying

Goal: Cancel common factors between numerators and denominators before multiplying.

1. Multiply: \( \frac{2}{3} \cdot \frac{3}{5} \)
Cancel the 3s: \( \frac{2}{\bcancel{3}} \cdot \frac{\bcancel{3}}{5} \)
Answer: \( \frac{2}{5} \)
2. Multiply: \( \frac{5}{8} \cdot \frac{4}{5} \)
Cancel the 5s. Reduce 4 and 8 (divide by 4):
\( \frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{1}{1} \)
Answer: \( \frac{1}{2} \)
3. Multiply: \( \frac{3}{4} \cdot \frac{8}{9} \)
Reduce 3 and 9 (to 1 and 3). Reduce 8 and 4 (to 2 and 1).
\( \frac{1}{1} \cdot \frac{2}{3} \)
Answer: \( \frac{2}{3} \)
4. Multiply: \( \frac{7}{10} \cdot \frac{5}{14} \)
Reduce 7 and 14 (to 1 and 2). Reduce 5 and 10 (to 1 and 2).
\( \frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{1}{2} \)
Answer: \( \frac{1}{4} \)
5. Multiply: \( \frac{6}{7} \cdot \frac{7}{12} \)
Cancel the 7s. Reduce 6 and 12 (to 1 and 2).
Answer: \( \frac{1}{2} \)

Topic #7: Finding Midpoints

Goal: Find the number exactly halfway between two others (find the average).

1. What number is halfway between 0 and 1?
Average: \( \frac{0+1}{2} \)
Answer: \( \frac{1}{2} \) (or 0.5)
2. What number is halfway between \( \frac{1}{3} \) and \( \frac{2}{3} \)?
Numerator: \( 1+2=3 \). Denominator stays 3. \( \frac{3}{3} = 1 \).
Divide by 2: \( 1 \div 2 \)
Answer: \( \frac{1}{2} \)
3. What number is halfway between \( \frac{1}{4} \) and \( \frac{3}{4} \)?
Add: \( \frac{1}{4} + \frac{3}{4} = \frac{4}{4} = 1 \).
Divide by 2: \( 1 \div 2 \)
Answer: \( \frac{1}{2} \)
4. What number is halfway between 0.2 and 0.8?
Add: \( 0.2 + 0.8 = 1.0 \).
Divide by 2: \( 1.0 \div 2 \)
Answer: 0.5
5. What number is halfway between 2 and 4?
Add: \( 2 + 4 = 6 \). Divide by 2.
Answer: 3

Topic #13: Dividing by Decimals

Goal: Move the decimal point to make the divisor a whole number.

1. Calculate: \( 20 \div 0.5 \)
Move decimal right one spot for both: \( 200 \div 5 \).
Answer: 40
2. Calculate: \( 12 \div 0.4 \)
Move decimal: \( 120 \div 4 \).
Answer: 30
3. Calculate: \( 9 \div 0.3 \)
Move decimal: \( 90 \div 3 \).
Answer: 30
4. Calculate: \( 45 \div 0.9 \)
Move decimal: \( 450 \div 9 \).
Answer: 50
5. Calculate: \( 100 \div 0.2 \)
Move decimal: \( 1000 \div 2 \).
Answer: 500

Topic #19: Solving Fraction Equations

Goal: Isolate the variable by doing the inverse operation (adding).

1. Find \( x \): \( x - \frac{1}{3} = \frac{1}{2} \)
Add \( \frac{1}{3} \) to both sides. Common denominator is 6.
\( \frac{2}{6} + \frac{3}{6} \)
Answer: \( \frac{5}{6} \)
2. Find \( m \): \( m - \frac{1}{4} = \frac{3}{8} \)
Add \( \frac{1}{4} \) (which is \( \frac{2}{8} \)) to \( \frac{3}{8} \).
\( \frac{3}{8} + \frac{2}{8} \)
Answer: \( \frac{5}{8} \)
3. Find \( y \): \( y - \frac{2}{5} = \frac{1}{5} \)
Add \( \frac{2}{5} \) to \( \frac{1}{5} \).
Answer: \( \frac{3}{5} \)
4. Find \( k \): \( k - \frac{5}{6} = \frac{1}{3} \)
Convert \( \frac{1}{3} \) to \( \frac{2}{6} \). Add \( \frac{5}{6} \).
\( \frac{5}{6} + \frac{2}{6} = \frac{7}{6} \)
Answer: \( 1\frac{1}{6} \)
5. Find \( w \): \( w - \frac{1}{2} = \frac{1}{4} \)
Convert \( \frac{1}{2} \) to \( \frac{2}{4} \). Add to \( \frac{1}{4} \).
Answer: \( \frac{3}{4} \)

Topic #20: Geometry (Solids)

Goal: Identify faces, edges, and vertices of geometric solids.

1. How many faces does a Triangular Pyramid have?
It has 1 base (triangle) and 3 sides (triangles).
Answer: 4 faces
2. How many edges does a Cube have?
4 on top, 4 on bottom, 4 vertical.
Answer: 12 edges
3. How many vertices does a Rectangular Prism have?
These are the corners. 4 on top, 4 on bottom.
Answer: 8 vertices
4. How many faces does a Square Pyramid have?
1 square base + 4 triangular sides.
Answer: 5 faces
5. How many edges does a Triangular Prism have?
3 on one triangle base, 3 on the other, 3 connecting them.
Answer: 9 edges

Topic #21: Multiplying Mixed Numbers

Goal: Convert to improper fractions first, then multiply.

1. Multiply: \( 1\frac{1}{2} \times 1\frac{1}{3} \)
Convert: \( \frac{3}{2} \times \frac{4}{3} \)
Cancel 3s. Reduce 4 and 2.
Answer: 2
2. Multiply: \( 2\frac{1}{2} \times 1\frac{1}{5} \)
Convert: \( \frac{5}{2} \times \frac{6}{5} \)
Cancel 5s. Reduce 6 and 2.
Answer: 3
3. Multiply: \( 1\frac{1}{4} \times 2 \)
Convert: \( \frac{5}{4} \times \frac{2}{1} \)
Reduce 2 and 4. \( \frac{5}{2} \).
Answer: \( 2\frac{1}{2} \)
4. Multiply: \( 3\frac{1}{3} \times 1\frac{1}{2} \)
Convert: \( \frac{10}{3} \times \frac{3}{2} \)
Cancel 3s. Reduce 10 and 2.
Answer: 5
5. Multiply: \( 1\frac{2}{5} \times 2\frac{1}{2} \)
Convert: \( \frac{7}{5} \times \frac{5}{2} \)
Cancel 5s. Result \( \frac{7}{2} \).
Answer: \( 3\frac{1}{2} \)
