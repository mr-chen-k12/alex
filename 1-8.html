1. Division of Sum and Difference

When the sum of 1.3 and 1.2 is divided by the difference of 1.3 and 1.2, what is the quotient?

$$ \text{Sum} = 1.3 + 1.2 = 2.5 $$ $$ \text{Difference} = 1.3 - 1.2 = 0.1 $$ $$ \text{Quotient} = \frac{2.5}{0.1} = 25 $$ Answer: 25
2. William Shakespeare's Age

William Shakespeare was born in 1564 and died in 1616. How many years did he live?

$$ 1616 - 1564 = 52 $$ Answer: 52 years
3. Field Goal Distance

Duane kicked a 45-yard field goal. How many feet is 45 yards?

There are 3 feet in 1 yard. $$ 45 \text{ yards} \times 3 \frac{\text{feet}}{\text{yard}} = 135 \text{ feet} $$ Answer: 135 feet
4. Explain: Square as a Regular Quadrilateral

Why is a square a regular quadrilateral?

A regular polygon must have congruent sides and congruent angles. A square has 4 congruent sides and 4 congruent angles (90° each). Therefore, it fits the definition of a regular quadrilateral.
5. Regular Hexagon Perimeter

A regular hexagon has a perimeter of 36 inches. How long is each side?

A regular hexagon has 6 equal sides. $$ \frac{36 \text{ inches}}{6} = 6 \text{ inches} $$ Answer: 6 inches

Multiply the length by the width to find the area of the rectangle (Width: \( \frac{1}{2} \) in, Length: \( \frac{3}{4} \) in).

18. Conclude

Is every rectangle a parallelogram?

Yes. A parallelogram is defined as a quadrilateral with two pairs of parallel sides. Since every rectangle has two pairs of parallel sides, every rectangle is a parallelogram. Answer: Yes

27. Reduce Fraction

Reduce: \( \frac{2 \cdot 2 \cdot 2 \cdot 3 \cdot 3}{2 \cdot 2 \cdot 3 \cdot 5 \cdot 5} \)

Cancel common factors (two 2s and one 3): $$ \frac{\not{2} \cdot \not{2} \cdot 2 \cdot \not{3} \cdot 3}{\not{2} \cdot \not{2} \cdot \not{3} \cdot 5 \cdot 5} = \frac{2 \cdot 3}{5 \cdot 5} = \frac{6}{25} $$ Answer: \( \frac{6}{25} \)
