1. Sum vs. Product of Fractions

Concept: Find the difference between (A + B) and (A × B).

1.1) Find the difference between the sum and product of \(\frac{1}{3}\) and \(\frac{1}{6}\).
Answer: \(\frac{4}{9}\)
Sum: \(\frac{2}{6} + \frac{1}{6} = \frac{3}{6} = \frac{1}{2}\)
Product: \(\frac{1}{18}\)
Difference: \(\frac{9}{18} - \frac{1}{18} = \frac{8}{18} = \frac{4}{9}\)
1.2) Calculate: \((\frac{1}{4} + \frac{1}{5}) - (\frac{1}{4} \times \frac{1}{5})\).
Answer: \(\frac{2}{5}\)
Sum: \(\frac{9}{20}\) | Product: \(\frac{1}{20}\)
Difference: \(\frac{8}{20} = \frac{2}{5}\)
1.3) Find the difference between the sum and product of \(\frac{1}{2}\) and \(\frac{1}{5}\).
Answer: \(\frac{3}{5}\)
Sum: \(\frac{7}{10}\) | Product: \(\frac{1}{10}\)
Difference: \(\frac{6}{10} = \frac{3}{5}\)
1.4) Evaluate for \(\frac{2}{3}\) and \(\frac{1}{4}\): (Sum) - (Product).
Answer: \(\frac{3}{4}\)
Sum: \(\frac{11}{12}\) | Product: \(\frac{2}{12}\)
Difference: \(\frac{9}{12} = \frac{3}{4}\)
1.5) Find the difference between the sum and product of \(\frac{3}{4}\) and \(\frac{1}{2}\).
Answer: \(\frac{7}{8}\)
Sum: \(\frac{5}{4} = \frac{10}{8}\) | Product: \(\frac{3}{8}\)
Difference: \(\frac{7}{8}\)

2. Division of Mixed Numbers

Concept: Convert to improper fractions, then multiply by the reciprocal.

2.1) Calculate: \(1\frac{1}{4} \div 2\frac{1}{2}\)
Answer: \(\frac{1}{2}\)
\(\frac{5}{4} \div \frac{5}{2} = \frac{5}{4} \times \frac{2}{5} = \frac{10}{20}\)
2.2) Calculate: \(3\frac{1}{3} \div 1\frac{2}{3}\)
Answer: \(2\)
\(\frac{10}{3} \div \frac{5}{3} = \frac{10}{3} \times \frac{3}{5} = \frac{30}{15} = 2\)
2.3) Calculate: \(2\frac{1}{5} \div 1\frac{1}{10}\)
Answer: \(2\)
\(\frac{11}{5} \div \frac{11}{10} = \frac{11}{5} \times \frac{10}{11} = 2\)
2.4) Calculate: \(5\frac{1}{2} \div 1\frac{3}{8}\)
Answer: \(4\)
\(\frac{11}{2} \div \frac{11}{8} = \frac{11}{2} \times \frac{8}{11} = 4\)
2.5) Calculate: \(1\frac{7}{8} \div 1\frac{1}{4}\)
Answer: \(1\frac{1}{2}\)
\(\frac{15}{8} \div \frac{5}{4} = \frac{15}{8} \times \frac{4}{5} = \frac{3}{2}\)

3. Solving Mixed Number Equations

Concept: Isolate variables (e.g., \(m - A = B \rightarrow m = B + A\)).

3.1) Solve for \(m\): \(m - 2\frac{1}{2} = 3\frac{3}{4}\)
Answer: \(6\frac{1}{4}\)
\(m = 3\frac{3}{4} + 2\frac{2}{4} = 5\frac{5}{4}\)
3.2) Solve for \(x\): \(x - 4\frac{1}{3} = 1\frac{5}{6}\)
Answer: \(6\frac{1}{6}\)
\(x = 1\frac{5}{6} + 4\frac{2}{6} = 5\frac{7}{6}\)
3.3) Solve for \(y\): \(y - 1\frac{3}{8} = 2\frac{1}{2}\)
Answer: \(3\frac{7}{8}\)
\(y = 2\frac{4}{8} + 1\frac{3}{8} = 3\frac{7}{8}\)
3.4) Solve for \(a\): \(a - 5\frac{2}{5} = 3\frac{7}{10}\)
Answer: \(9\frac{1}{10}\)
\(a = 3\frac{7}{10} + 5\frac{4}{10} = 8\frac{11}{10}\)
3.5) Solve for \(b\): \(b - 3\frac{1}{4} = 1\frac{2}{3}\)
Answer: \(4\frac{11}{12}\)
\(b = 1\frac{8}{12} + 3\frac{3}{12} = 4\frac{11}{12}\)

4. Decimal Equations

Concept: Divide to isolate the variable.

4.1) Solve for \(d\): \(20d = 0.5\)
Answer: \(0.025\)
\(d = 0.5 \div 20 = 0.025\)
4.2) Solve for \(x\): \(0.4x = 1.2\)
Answer: \(3\)
\(x = 1.2 \div 0.4 = 12 \div 4 = 3\)
4.3) Solve for \(y\): \(15y = 4.5\)
Answer: \(0.3\)
\(y = 4.5 \div 15 = 0.3\)
4.4) Solve for \(z\): \(0.05z = 10\)
Answer: \(200\)
\(z = 10 \div 0.05 = 1000 \div 5 = 200\)
4.5) Solve for \(m\): \(50m = 0.1\)
Answer: \(0.002\)
\(m = 0.1 \div 50 = 0.002\)

6. Elapsed Time

Concept: Calculate duration between start and end times.

6.1) Sunrise was at 6:15 a.m. and sunset was at 7:30 p.m. How long was the day?
Answer: 13 hours, 15 minutes
6:15 am to 6:15 pm = 12 hours.
6:15 pm to 7:30 pm = 1 hour 15 minutes.
6.2) A movie started at 1:45 p.m. and ended at 4:10 p.m. How long was it?
Answer: 2 hours, 25 minutes
1:45 to 3:45 = 2 hours.
3:45 to 4:10 = 25 minutes.
6.3) School starts at 8:10 a.m. and ends at 2:50 p.m. Duration?
Answer: 6 hours, 40 minutes
8:10 to 2:10 = 6 hours.
2:10 to 2:50 = 40 minutes.
6.4) A train departed at 10:30 a.m. and arrived at 1:15 p.m. Trip time?
Answer: 2 hours, 45 minutes
10:30 to 12:30 = 2 hours.
12:30 to 1:15 = 45 minutes.
6.5) A shift is 7:55 a.m. to 4:25 p.m. Total time?
Answer: 8 hours, 30 minutes
7:55 to 3:55 = 8 hours.
3:55 to 4:25 = 30 minutes.
